# What is this?

Collection of notes on math for machine learning.
Not intended to be comprehensive just a quick reference for me. Mostly just misconceptions and things I forget.

## Inline Math
Here’s an example of **inline math**:
`$E = mc^2$` renders as:

$E = mc^2$

## Block Math
Use double dollar signs `$$ ... $$` for block math:

$$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} \,dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$
